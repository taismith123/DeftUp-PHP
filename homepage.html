WELCOME TO DEFTUP

DeftUp provides people with assistance in certain jobs/tasks that they can't get to or do not know how to accomplish. This will include tasks like around-the-house yardwork, mounting, furniture assembly and much more. This website will create more jobs for 1-person businesses and provide the consumers that are hiring to get tasks done with more freedom and flexibility to take care of other jobs/tasks that they have.
